Transforming Laerdal Medical's Support Experience

Overview

Laerdal Medical's new Support and Help solution is designed to empower customers with a seamless, self-service platform for troubleshooting and resolving issues efficiently. The primary goal is to reduce dependency on support teams while improving customer satisfaction and response times. This solution includes both embedded and standalone versions, with the current focus on the standalone version to enhance accessibility and usability.

My Role

As the lead UX designer, I was responsible for crafting an intuitive and efficient user experience. My contributions included:

  • Conducted in-depth user research to uncover pain points.
  • Defined user flows and restructured information architecture.
  • Designed interactive prototypes and tested them with real users.
  • Collaborated with stakeholders, developers, and support teams.

The Challenge

High Support Dependency

Customers struggled to find answers independently, leading to frustration and increased reliance on Laerdal’s help desk. In many cases, their first point of contact was a salesperson, who then redirected them to the support team—unnecessarily prolonging resolution times.

Lack of Proactive Support

Support materials were often insufficient or difficult to navigate. Given Laerdal’s extensive product range, customers found it challenging to locate relevant information for troubleshooting or best practices.

Problem Statement

How might we design a self-service support experience that reduces help desk dependency and empowers customers to resolve issues efficiently?

Research & Discovery

Key Research Questions

  • Who are our primary users, and what are their biggest challenges?
  • How do they currently seek support, and what obstacles do they face?
  • What key features would make the support experience more effective?

Methodologies Used

  • ✅ Competitive Analysis – Evaluated best practices from leading support solutions.
  • ✅ User Interviews – Gathered insights from customers on their support experiences.
  • ✅ Ecosystem Mapping – Analyzed Laerdal’s digital landscape to ensure seamless integration.
  • ✅ User Personas & Journeys - Defined key user types and their specific needs.
  • ✅ Data Analysis - Gathered and analised existing data for prioritisation and set foundation for future trackings.
  • ✅ Task Flows & Pain Points - Identified friction points in the current support experience.

Design Approach

  • 1. Intuitive Navigation & Search · Content prioritisation, streamlined information architecture and improved search functionality ensure users can quickly find relevant resources without frustration.
  • 2. Guided Troubleshooting · Step-by-step troubleshooting guides and personalized recommendations empower users to resolve issues independently.
  • 3. Smart Case Creation · An optimized case submission process gathers all necessary details upfront, reducing back-and-forth communication and improving response times.
  • 4. Feedback options · Implemented system for feedback gathering and future improvements.

Prototyping & Iteration

We followed an iterative design process to refine the solution:

  • Wireframes & Prototypes · Created interactive prototypes to visualize key interactions.
  • Testing · Conducted tests with real users to gather feedback.
  • Design Refinements · Iterated based on testing insights to enhance clarity and efficiency.

Key Learnings

  • ✅ User-Centered Design · Direct user engagement shaped our approach and design decisions.
  • ✅ Data-Driven Iteration · Continuous testing and feedback loops ensured usability improvements.
  • ✅ Cross-Team Collaboration · Close coordination with stakeholders enabled a solution that met both user and business needs.

Next Steps & Future Improvements

The project is currently in its implementation phase, with ongoing enhancements:

  • Additional user testing to validate and refine the experience.
  • Further optimization of search and AI-powered recommendations.
  • Monitoring KPIs post-launch to measure impact and inform future iterations.

Conclusion

By transforming Laerdal Medical’s support experience, this new solution will reduce reliance on the help desk and enhance self-service capabilities for customers.
